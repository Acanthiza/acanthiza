Front chain ring for 1*11-46

Given an 11-46 cluster, what is a good front chain ring for a 1-by setup?

Figure 1 shows front chain ring, rear cog, speed and cadence change in relation to an upper and lower speed target. Targets were chosen as 6 km/hr as close to walking speed and 45 km/hr as it’s rare I travel faster than that.

Cadence was chosen in steps of 10 between 50 rpm and 110 rpm.

Any front chain ring between 36 and 40 cross both lines, given the above constraints. For some reason I’m favouring high-end speed over low-end cadence so I think I’ll pick 42.
